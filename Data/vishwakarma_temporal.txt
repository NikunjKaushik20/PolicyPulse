Year 2023:
PM Vishwakarma was launched on Vishwakarma Jayanti (September 17) for traditional artisans and craftspeople. Scheme covers 18 trades including carpentry, blacksmithy, goldsmith, pottery, masonry, tailoring. Recognition through PM Vishwakarma Certificate and ID card. Collateral-free loans up to Rs 3 lakh at 5% interest. Free toolkit, skill training, and marketing support provided.

Year 2024:
First full year saw 50 lakh artisans registered. Rs 5,000 crore disbursed in credit support. Skill training through ITIs and specialized centers. Digital marketplace integration through GeM. Women artisans received priority with 40% share.

Year 2025:
Target of 1 crore artisans under scheme. Credit limit enhanced to Rs 5 lakh for proven performers. Export support for high-quality craftwork. Cluster development for traditional craft villages. GI tagging support for unique crafts.
