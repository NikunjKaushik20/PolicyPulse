Year 2022:
Prime Minister's Development Initiative for North East Region (PM-DevINE) was launched with Rs 6,600 crore allocation. Focus on infrastructure, employment, and social development in Northeast states. Special emphasis on connectivity and tourism. Youth livelihood projects prioritized. State-specific projects based on development gaps.

Year 2023:
Rs 2,000 crore projects sanctioned. Bamboo-based industries developed. Tourism infrastructure enhanced. Educational institutions upgraded. Healthcare facilities improved in remote areas.

Year 2025:
Northeast development indicators improved. Connectivity with mainland strengthened. Youth employment opportunities created. Cultural tourism thriving. Region becomes growth engine.
