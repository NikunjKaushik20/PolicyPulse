Year 2020:
Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana was launched to transform fisheries sector. Rs 20,050 crore scheme over 5 years. Focus on marine, inland, and aquaculture development. Infrastructure for fishing harbors and cold chain. Employment target of 55 lakh in fisheries.

Year 2022:
Fish production reached 16 million tonnes. 2 lakh new fish farmers enrolled. Aquaculture area expanded 20%. Fishing vessel modernization accelerated. Fish export touched $8 billion.

Year 2025:
Fish production target of 22 million tonnes. Employment generation 40 lakh cumulative. India third largest fish exporter globally. Sustainable fishing practices mainstreamed. Blue economy growth demonstrated.
