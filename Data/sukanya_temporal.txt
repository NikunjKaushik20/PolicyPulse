Year 2015:
Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana was launched as part of Beti Bachao Beti Padhao to secure the financial future of girl children. The scheme offers highest small savings interest rate at 9.1% initially. Parents can open account for girl child below 10 years with minimum Rs 250 deposit. Maximum annual deposit of Rs 1.5 lakh with tax benefits under 80C. Account matures after 21 years or on marriage after 18.

Year 2016:
1.26 crore accounts opened with deposits exceeding Rs 8,000 crore. Interest rate adjusted to 8.6% but remained highest among small savings. Partial withdrawal allowed for higher education after age 18. Banks and post offices both enabled as account platforms. Rural enrollment campaigns through gram panchayats.

Year 2017:
Accounts crossed 1.5 crore with total deposits Rs 25,000 crore. Interest rate reduced to 8.3% aligning with market conditions. Aadhaar linkage made mandatory for large deposits. Digital passbook facility launched. NRI accounts discontinued for resident-only scheme.

Year 2018:
Interest rate stabilized at 8.1%. Total accounts reached 1.8 crore. Average deposit per account Rs 15,000 showed genuine saving behavior. Integration with Sukanya Samriddhi calculators in bank apps. Maturity payouts began for earliest opened accounts.

Year 2019:
2 crore accounts milestone reached. Quarterly interest rate revision aligned with market. Deposits crossed Rs 50,000 crore. Online account opening through post office savings bank. Single girl child received additional quota.

Year 2020:
COVID impacted new enrollments but existing deposits remained strong. Interest rate maintained at 7.6% despite rate cuts elsewhere. Digital deposit mechanisms gained importance. 2.3 crore accounts with Rs 65,000 crore deposits. Minimum deposit grace period extended.

Year 2021:
Recovery in new account opening. Interest rate adjusted to 7.6%. Total deposits exceeded Rs 80,000 crore. UPI deposits integrated with accounts. Investment planning tools for parents launched.

Year 2022:
2.8 crore accounts achieved. Interest rate competitive at 7.6% compared to FDs. Financial literacy programs for enrolled families. Early maturity statistics showed 95% completion rates. Second girl child enrollment incentivized.

Year 2023:
3 crore accounts milestone crossed. Deposits touched Rs 1 lakh crore. Interest rate steady at 8.0% after revision. Scholarship integration for account holders explored. Insurance add-on products planned.

Year 2024:
Election year saw promotional campaigns. Account opening simplified with e-KYC. 3.3 crore accounts. Interest rate increased to 8.2%. First batch large-scale maturities for education funding.

Year 2025:
Near-saturation in urban areas with rural focus. Total deposits Rs 1.25 lakh crore. AI-based investment planning for parents. Insurance integration for comprehensive girl child security. International remittance for NRI grandparents.
