Year 2020:
PM Street Vendor's AtmaNirbhar Nidhi (PM SVANidhi) was launched during COVID to provide working capital loans to street vendors. Collateral-free loans of Rs 10,000 initially offered. Interest subsidy of 7% provided by government. Digital payments incentives through cashback. Letter of Recommendation from ULBs required for eligibility.

Year 2021:
Scheme expanded with Rs 20,000 second tranche for good repayers. 25 lakh vendors enrolled with Rs 2,500 crore disbursed. Digital onboarding through SVANidhi mobile app. Repayment rates exceeded 80% showing credit discipline. Integration with e-Shram for informal worker registration.

Year 2022:
Third tranche of Rs 50,000 introduced. 35 lakh unique vendors benefited. Family health insurance and scholarship for children added. Street vendor survey completed in most cities. NPA rates remained below 5%.

Year 2023:
40 lakh cumulative beneficiaries. Fintech partnerships expanded lending reach. Vendor digitization with QR codes reached 30 lakh. Credit graduation to formal banking explored. Street vending zones formalized in many cities.

Year 2024:
Election year saw enhanced focus. 50 lakh vendor target approached. Enhanced interest subsidy for women vendors. Insurance coverage expanded. Vendor markets developed in urban areas.

Year 2025:
Universal street vendor coverage in urban areas. Credit scores for vendors enabling higher loans. E-commerce integration for online sales. Social security benefits linked to SVANidhi. Formalization of informal sector advancing.
