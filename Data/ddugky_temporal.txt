Year 2014:
Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Grameen Kaushalya Yojana (DDU-GKY) was launched for rural youth skill development. Focus on placement-linked training for 15-35 years age group. Minimum 75% placement guarantee from training providers. Priority for SC/ST/OBC and women candidates. Post-placement support for retention.

Year 2018:
10 lakh youth trained and placed. 700+ training partners operational. Overseas placement initiated. Success in hospitality and retail sectors. State Rural Livelihoods Missions as implementation arm.

Year 2020:
COVID disrupted training and placement. Online training modules developed. Placement rate dropped temporarily. Healthcare sector training prioritized. Skills for self-employment added.

Year 2023:
20 lakh cumulative trained and placed. Apprenticeship integration strengthened. Industry-specific skilling enhanced. Recognition of Prior Learning expanded. Wage employment sustainability improved.

Year 2025:
30 lakh skilled rural youth. Skill credits and digital certificates enabled. Overseas employment partnerships expanded. Manufacturing sector placements grew. Rural transformation through skills.
