Year 2014:
Mission Indradhanush was launched to expand immunization coverage to all children under 2 and pregnant women. Target to achieve 90% full immunization coverage from 65% baseline. Focus on 201 high-priority districts with low coverage. Four rounds of intensive immunization drives per year. Vaccines against 7 diseases: diphtheria, pertussis, tetanus, polio, measles, TB, hepatitis B.

Year 2015:
First year covered 352 districts. 2.1 crore children vaccinated. Full immunization coverage improved to 70%. Rotavirus vaccine added in phased manner. Sustained communication campaigns for awareness.

Year 2016:
Extended to all 696 districts. Measles-Rubella vaccine introduced. Pneumococcal vaccine added in high-burden states. Cold chain infrastructure strengthened. ASHA worker incentives enhanced.

Year 2017:
Intensified Mission Indradhanush (IMI) launched. Full immunization target of 90% by December 2018. Focus on persistent low-coverage pockets. Door-to-door search for missed children. 497 lakh additional beneficiaries covered.

Year 2018:
New vaccines: rotavirus, pneumococcal, measles-rubella nationally. Full immunization reached 85%. Left-out and drop-out children identified systematically. Birth dose hepatitis B improved. Cold chain expansion to last mile.

Year 2019:
IMI 2.0 launched for remaining gaps. Full immunization at 90% in many states. Universal immunization claimed substantial achievement. Japanese Encephalitis vaccine in endemic areas. Data-driven identification of missed children.

Year 2020:
COVID severely disrupted routine immunization. Catch-up campaigns post-lockdown. COVID vaccine infrastructure built on Indradhanush foundation. Full immunization dropped to 85% temporarily. Essential immunization resumed with protocols.

Year 2021:
IMI 3.0 launched to recover lost ground. COVID vaccination leveraged Indradhanush infrastructure. Childhood immunization recovering. New vaccines under evaluation. Cold chain now COVID-ready.

Year 2022:
Full immunization returned to 90%+ levels. IMI 4.0 focuses on sustained coverage. Cold chain modernization completed. Electronic Vaccine Intelligence Network operational. Zero-dose children tracked.

Year 2023:
Intensified Mission Indradhanush 5.0 for gaps. Full immunization 92% nationally. New vaccine pipeline planned. State-level micro-plans refined. Equity focus on marginalized communities.

Year 2024:
Election year focus on maternal-child health. Full immunization targets exceeded in most states. New cancer vaccine under consideration. Mobile immunization units expanded. Target 95% full immunization.

Year 2025:
Universal immunization substantially achieved. India achieves WHO 90-90 targets. Child mortality reduction documented. Vaccine hesitancy addressed comprehensively. World recognition for immunization success.
