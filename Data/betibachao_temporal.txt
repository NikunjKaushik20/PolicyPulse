Year 2015:
Beti Bachao Beti Padhao was launched on January 22, 2015 from Panipat, Haryana to address declining child sex ratio (CSR). The scheme targeted districts with CSR below 918 initially covering 100 districts. Focus was on preventing gender-biased sex selection and ensuring survival and education of girl child. Multi-sectoral action coordinated by Women & Child Development, Health, and Education ministries. Community mobilization and awareness campaigns were central to the strategy.

Year 2016:
Scheme expanded to 61 additional districts based on CSR data. Child sex ratio at birth improved in 104 of 161 target districts. Anti-natal sex determination enforcement strengthened. School enrollment of girls showed measurable improvement. Celebrity ambassadors promoted scheme messaging nationally.

Year 2017:
All 640 districts brought under BBBP coverage. Sex ratio at birth improved from 918 to 923 nationally. Girls' enrollment in secondary education increased by 5%. Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana linked for girl child financial security. Gram Panchayats recognized for improving girl child outcomes.

Year 2018:
Focus shifted from awareness to measurable outcomes. Sex ratio at birth reached 929 showing sustained improvement. Toilet construction for girls in schools reached near-saturation. Self-defense training programs for girls expanded. District collectors made accountable for CSR improvement.

Year 2019:
Scheme restructured with emphasis on education and empowerment. Sex ratio at birth touched 934 in focused districts. Scholarship programs for meritorious girls strengthened. Menstrual hygiene programs integrated with BBBP. Sports and skill development for girls prioritized.

Year 2020:
COVID impacted scheme activities and monitoring. Virtual campaigns replaced physical awareness programs. Helplines for girls facing domestic issues received increased calls. Sex ratio improvement momentum slowed during pandemic. Digital education support prioritized for girls.

Year 2021:
Scheme activities resumed with enhanced focus. Sex ratio at birth nationally reached 940. Girls' enrollment in higher education showed significant gains. Integration with Poshan Abhiyaan for nutrition outcomes. Mission Shakti announced as expanded women empowerment umbrella.

Year 2022:
Mission Shakti subsumes BBBP components. Sex ratio continues improvement trend at 945. Focus on preventing child marriage intensified. Skill development for adolescent girls expanded. STEM education for girls promoted.

Year 2023:
Under Mission Shakti, BBBP outcomes tracked rigorously. Sex ratio at birth reaches 950 in many states. Girls' toilet facility reaches 100% in schools. Cyber safety awareness for girls integrated. Women helpline expanded with technology.

Year 2024:
Sex ratio at birth nationally approaches 955. Early marriage prevention shows measurable decline. Girls' participation in competitive exams increases. Digital literacy programs target rural girls. BBBP messaging integrated with electoral awareness.

Year 2025:
Near-natural sex ratio achieved in many districts. Girl child education to graduation prioritized. Financial independence programs for young women expanded. AI-based monitoring of scheme outcomes deployed. International recognition for gender equality progress.
