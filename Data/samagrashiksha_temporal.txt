Year 2018:
Samagra Shiksha was launched integrating SSA, RMSA, and Teacher Education schemes. Comprehensive approach from pre-school to class 12. Focus on quality education through improved learning outcomes. Digital infrastructure and smart classrooms promoted. Teacher training and professional development prioritized.

Year 2020:
COVID disrupted physical schooling. Digital learning platforms launched under Samagra Shiksha. DIKSHA and SWAYAM content expanded. Learning loss documentation initiated. Mid-day meal converted to take-home rations.

Year 2023:
NEP 2020 implementation through Samagra framework. Foundational literacy and numeracy focus through NIPUN Bharat. Vocational education integration from class 6. Mother tongue instruction in early grades. School infrastructure significantly upgraded.

Year 2025:
Foundational learning outcomes dramatically improved. Digital learning normalized in rural schools. Teacher quality enhanced through continuous training. School-to-work transitions strengthened. India improves in global education rankings.
