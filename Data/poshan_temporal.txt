Year 2018:
POSHAN Abhiyaan (Prime Minister's Overarching Scheme for Holistic Nourishment) was launched to address malnutrition through lifecycle approach. Target to reduce stunting, underweight, anemia and low birth weight. All 36 States and UTs covered with Jan Andolan approach. Technology platform for real-time monitoring through ICDS-CAS. Convergence with Swachh Bharat, drinking water, and health schemes.

Year 2019:
National targets: 2% reduction per year in stunting, undernutrition, anemia. Measurable decline in stunting from 38% to 35% observed. Anganwadi workers trained in using growth monitoring devices. POSHAN Maah observed in September for awareness. Behavioral change communication campaigns intensified.

Year 2020:
COVID severely impacted nutrition service delivery. Take-home rations replaced hot-cooked meals at Anganwadis. POSHAN Tracker app launched for monitoring. Nutrition rehabilitation centers continued in modified form. Emergency supplementary nutrition provided to vulnerable groups.

Year 2021:
Mission POSHAN 2.0 launched integrating supplementary nutrition and POSHAN Abhiyaan. Focus on maternal nutrition and early childhood care intensified. Anganwadi infrastructure strengthening for better service delivery. Stunting reduction target maintained despite pandemic setback. POSHAN Vatika (nutrition gardens) promoted.

Year 2022:
Comprehensive POSHAN 2.0 implementation across country. Fortified rice distribution integrated with PDS. Anemia reduction campaigns targeting adolescent girls. Early childhood development focus beyond nutrition. Millets promotion for nutritional security.

Year 2023:
Stunting reduced to 32% showing significant progress. Severe acute malnutrition cases reduced by 40%. Community-based management of malnutrition strengthened. POSHAN Tracker coverage expanded to all Anganwadis. International recognition for reduction achievements.

Year 2024:
Election year focus on nutrition outcomes. Free food grains under NFSA extended. Anganwadi worker incentives enhanced. Technology integration for monitoring strengthened. National targets approaching achievement.

Year 2025:
Near-target achievement for stunting reduction. Anemia still needs focused intervention. Comprehensive early childhood development framework. AI-powered nutrition monitoring piloted. SDG 2 (Zero Hunger) targets progressing.
