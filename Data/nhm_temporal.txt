Year 2013:
National Health Mission was restructured combining NRHM and NUHM for comprehensive health coverage. Focus on maternal and child health, communicable diseases, and health infrastructure. Free drugs and diagnostics as key service delivery component. Health and Wellness Centers for primary care. Ayushman Bharat later integrated with NHM framework.

Year 2018:
1.5 lakh Health and Wellness Centers target set. Free essential medicines available at 50,000 facilities. Janani Suraksha Yojana under NHM crossed 3 crore beneficiaries. Immunization campaigns achieved record coverage. Mobile Medical Units expanded rural outreach.

Year 2020:
COVID response integrated with NHM infrastructure. Health workers under NHM formed pandemic frontline. Teleconsultation services launched under e-Sanjeevani. Testing and treatment infrastructure expanded. 3 lakh ASHAs supported COVID surveillance.

Year 2023:
Health and Wellness Centers reached 1.5 lakh target. Digital health records under ABHA integrated. Specialist telemedicine available at district level. NCD screening coverage expanded. Mental health services added to NHM basket.

Year 2025:
Near-universal PHC coverage through HWCs. AI-powered diagnostic support at primary level. Integrated health and wellness approach. NHM forms backbone of universal health coverage. India health outcomes dramatically improved.
